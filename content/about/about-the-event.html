About The Event

The Seminole Hard Rock Hotel & Casino Hollywood will be the setting for the Diversity Honors gala that kicks off Harvey Milk Day events across the nation and will benefit the Harvey Milk Foundation and The Pride Center at Equality Park. This highly anticipated event on the eve of Saturday, April 1, 2023, will showcase those who are transforming lives by living authentically and advancing inclusiveness.

This annual event is where the community can come together to honor men and women who, in their extraordinary lives, remind us all of the diversity of the human spirit and the values that define us. This collaborative effort between the Harvey Milk Foundation, The Pride Center at Equality Park and the Seminole Hard Rock Hotel and Casino is an ongoing celebration of those who exemplify authenticity, leadership and courage.
